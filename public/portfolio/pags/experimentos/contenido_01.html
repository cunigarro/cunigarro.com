Pixel Painter

This application was created in javascript together with a library called processing.js and the tag canvas from html5, which brings the concept of the pixel to generate an illustration tool. To draw it is necessary to click on the points that are on the black background and to change the color, it is only necessary to combine the three colors located at the bottom, with a right click increase the value and with the left click decrease.
