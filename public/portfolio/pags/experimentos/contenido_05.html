Ciclo

Ciclo tries to link human thoughts and feelings to the seasons of nature, although distant for us who are in Colombia, we can be aware of what they are and represent. The user could then send a message with a specific station and these in turn were projected on a building to which several people were witnesses.

The project reached the Wizard of Oz stage but we managed to test it with real users and the experience was very well received.
