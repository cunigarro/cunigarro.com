Cinemática programada

A simple exercise on moving objects in which the circles gradually lose their ability to bounce. By clicking on the animation we will see how the animation restarts and the spheres lose their rebound force. This exercise was programmed in javascript using a library called processing.js and the tag canvas from html5.
