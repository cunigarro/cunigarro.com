Flash self-assessment activities

Below you will find a series of self-evaluation activities designed for virtual courses, some are simple and others have a bit of complexity, of which you can leave me questions in the comments space, all are elaborated in Action Script 2, however I will program them soon in Action Script 3, they are all downloadable and free to use. Through time I will be creating new activities and sharing them.

Activities developed in Action Script 2:

  1. Concentrate.
  2. Drag and drop.
  3. Match with lines.
  4. True and false.
  5. Sort text.
  6. Multiple choice with only answer.
