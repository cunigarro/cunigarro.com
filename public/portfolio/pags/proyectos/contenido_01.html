ENTOMUSEO

Entomuseo (Museum of Insects) brings together an exhaustive photographic work where panoramic views of the habitats of insects were developed and an application that allows creating a three-dimensional view of it through photographic shots from all its angles. Below I share the abstract of the project:

In order to preserve and disseminate knowledge, museums must have adequate means to achieve their objectives. But having appropriate spaces implies not only having conservation and exhibition areas, but also adequate infrastructure for it, without counting on the availability of guides and security personnel that together involve high investments in development, implementation and subsequent maintenance.

To overcome these difficulties, many museums are using Information and Communication Technologies (ICT) tools which allow the visitor to view their collections and exhibitions, learn historical details and context. This project was based on granting these tools for the conservation and dissemination of the Entomological collection of the National University Sede Palmira, also looking for attractive and innovative ways to get this information, which takes advantage of the potential offered by diversity and conservation care. that has the collection.

  • Project manager: Boris Villamil Ramirez.
  • Photography equipment designer: Iván Ocampo Triviño.
  • Interface design and programming: Christian Unigarro Diaz.
